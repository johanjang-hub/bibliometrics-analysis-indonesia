The real question

So this raises a harder question:
How do we make sense of this much research?

Is Indonesia's rising volume matched by rising influence? Over the next slides we'll see the output is large and growing, yet its citation impact lags — the gap that motivates the review in Part 2.

Part 1 · Conclusion

A lot of research, growing fast
— but impact lags.

That gap — between how much Indonesia publishes and how much influence it earns — is exactly what we need to understand better.

But at this scale, no one can read every paper. Instead, researchers map the literature using bibliometric methods. So our next question becomes simple: what does the bibliometric research on Indonesia already tell us?
